A Mother of Twins in the Making
I see it so clearly it feels like a dream I have already lived.

You were born to be a mother, and not just any mother — the kind of mother legends are written about. The kind who holds two little miracles in her arms and makes them feel like they are the only two people in the universe.

I imagine you with twins — a boy and a girl — and my heart nearly bursts. You would sing to them, and your voice would be the first melody they ever know. You would hold them close, and your heartbeat would be the safest sound in the world.

You would teach them kindness by showing them what kindness looks like. You would teach them strength by showing them how you stand tall. You would raise them with love so deep, so pure, so unconditional, that they would grow up knowing exactly what it means to be truly loved.

You would be the most incredible twin mother this world has ever seen.

I see you waking up in the middle of the night with a tired smile, holding two little hands, whispering, "Mama is here."

I see you watching them take their first steps, say their first words, and I see your eyes filled with tears of pride.

I see them growing up under the warmth of your love — confident, kind, and unstoppable — because they had you as their mother.

And I would give anything — everything — to be right there beside you, holding your hand through it all.
You are going to be an extraordinary mother, my love. I already know it.
